Frequently Asked Questions

What is Connext and how does it work?

Connext is a platform that connects investors with promising startups. Investors can explore startups, review their profiles, and invest directly.

Is investing in startups risky?

Yes, startup investments involve risks, including market fluctuations and business failures. We encourage investors to conduct thorough research before investing.

How do I find the right startup to invest in?

You can filter startups by industry, funding stage, and location. We also provide key metrics to help investors make informed decisions.

What are the fees for investors?

We do not charge investors any fees for browsing startups. However, investment transactions may include platform or legal processing fees.
